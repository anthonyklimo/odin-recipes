Som Tum

(Green Papaya Salad)

Description

Som tum is a spicy salad of shredded green papaya, tomatoes, green beans, and other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1/4 cup palm sugar
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1-4 Thai bird chilies
  • 2 tbsp dried shrimp
  • 1/2 cup green beans cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 3 cups shredded green papaya
  • 8 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts

Directions

  1. Combine fish sauce, sugar, and lime juice and mix well
  2. In a mortar, lightly pound garlic, chilies, and dried shrimp until broken down. Add green beans and pound lightly
  3. Add green papaya and cherry tomatoes. Pound lightly to bruise vegetables and release juices
  4. Pour fish sauce, sugar, lime dressing over salad and mix well.
  5. Stir in roasted peanutes
  6. Adjust seasoning as needed
