Tom Kha Gai

(Thai Coconut Chicken Soup)

Description

Tom Kha Gai is a hot a sour coconut based soup with chicken. "Gai" in Thai
means chicken, but Tom Kha is not limited to Gai, i.e. Tom Kha Goong (shrimp)
is a delicious alternative, so feel free to make any Tom Kha you like!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 1 lemongrass stalk
  • 10 slices galangal
  • 6 kaffir lime leaves
  • 12 oz. chicken breast or thigh, sliced
  • 1 cup fresh mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon chili oil (nam prik pao)
  • 1-4 Thai bird chilis (to taste)
  • 2 tbsp cilantro (chopped)

Steps

  1. In a pot, combine coconut milk, chicken stock, lemongrass, galangal, kaffir lime leaves,
    and sliced chicken; bring to a boil then reduce to a simmer for a few minutes
  2. Add mushrooms and continue cooking until chicken is cooked through
  3. return to boil, add fish sauce, lime joice, chili oil, and chilis
  4. Remove from heat and adjust seasoning as needed
  5. Serve garnished with cilantro
